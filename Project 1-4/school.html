My Education

Some Backgroud...

I attended UCLA for undergrad from 2014-2018. In the beginning I really struggled to adjust to the new environment since I was the first in my family to ever attend a four year university. There were many challenges I faced going into this portion of my life. It was the first time ever that I was required to be responsible for my own learning outcomes and adhere to some sort of personal accountability. It wasn't easy and there were many classes that challenged me to a point that I was unable to handle. I had originally planned on studying and majoring in the physical sciences but eventually settled on majoring in political science. After learning many lessons in and out of the lecture hall, I ultimately prevailed and graduated in the summer of 2018. Since then I had always wanted to pursue a career in programming but never had the time to enroll in traditional schooling since I was working and building a recording studio business. At the beginning of 2023 I was made aware that Cerritos College had a programming program that was entirely online, so I enrolled. I'm currently in the middle of a 1 year programming Associates program that will help me build a foundation for my future profession in the tech industry.
